About the Home

A rare escape above the clouds

Nestled within a private community, Knobview Retreat sits at 2,900 feet elevation in the Blue Ridge Mountains — offering sweeping panoramic views that change with every season and every hour of the day.

Whether you're here for adventure or restoration, this thoughtfully appointed home delivers both. Step outside to hike world-class trails, soak in the hot tub under a star-filled sky, or simply settle into the deep quiet that only mountain air can provide.

Meet Your Hosts

We are Steve and Henrietta Holbrooks, and our roots in North Carolina run deep. Having spent decades hiking the trails and cruising the Blue Ridge Parkway, we've developed a profound appreciation for the serenity and beauty of these mountains.

After our first stay at Knob View in August 2013, we knew we had found something special. The property's unique tranquility and stunning views offered a peace we hadn't found anywhere else. Whether we are out kayaking or exploring local mountain heritage, our goal is to provide a stay that reflects the same warmth and adventure we've enjoyed here for years. We invite you to experience the best of the Blue Ridge from a home that holds a very special place in our hearts.
